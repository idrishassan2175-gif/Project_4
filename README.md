# Project_4
Financial Analysis Report

built this Financial Analysis Dashboard in Power BI to better understand how sales, profit, and customer segments contribute to overall business performance.

🔍 What I focused on

● Tracking key KPIs: Sales, Orders, Profit, Profit Margin, and Discounts

● Comparing current vs prior year performance
● Breaking down performance by country, segment, and product

📈 Key takeaways

● Sales and profit show strong year-over-year growth
● Profitability varies across countries, suggesting different market dynamics
● High discounts are common and may be impacting margins
● Distributors are the most profitable segment, while corporate underperforms
● A few products drive most of the revenue

🛠️ Tools used

Power BI, DAX, and data modeling
